ABOUT ME

Hi My Name is Thuy Nguyen

I was born in Vietnam and raised in Houston. I attended the University of Houston and earned a Bachelor degree in Finance and Marketing from the Bauer Colllege of Business. After university, I gained experiences in Accounting, Marketing and Business Development with local companies. As a daughter of immigrant parents, I was taught to always to be curious, creative, and to seek knowledge. The blending of cultures and the diversity of Houston has led to my passions for food, culture and baking.

My work experience, creativity, and thirst for knowledge led me to Web Development at The Iron Yard. There, I went through an intensive Front-End Developement immersion course focusing on Javascript. With my new found web developement skills, I am able to solve problems and create applications to enhance user experiences.
